Brief Bio

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Rutgers University. My research spans large (vision) language models, computer vision, and AI trustworthiness.

Previously, I have been a Research Scientist at Google, a Core Faculty Member at MILA, and an Assistant Professor at McGill. I completed my Ph.D. at Columbia University, advised by Carl Vondrick and Junfeng Yang.

I am actively recruiting highly self-motivated Ph.D. students, research interns, and postdocs. If you’re interested, please email me with your CV and a brief note on how your background.
